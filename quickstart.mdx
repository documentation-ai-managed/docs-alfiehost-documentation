## Overview

Set up your Alfie Host guest manual in under 10 minutes. This digital guide answers guest questions instantly via natural language chat, covering check-in details, property features, and local tips. Follow these steps to create your account, complete the setup wizard, add property information, and test the interface.

<Callout kind="tip">
  You need a valid email address and property details ready, such as address and amenities.
</Callout>

## Setup Steps

Use these numbered steps to launch your first guest manual.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [app.alfiehost.com/signup](https://app.alfiehost.com/signup) and sign up with your email.

    Enter your hosting business name, like "Cozy Cabins LLC", and create a strong password.

    Verify your email via the confirmation link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Initial Setup Wizard" icon="sparkles">
    After login, the wizard guides you through essentials.

    Select your property type (e.g., apartment, vacation home) and time zone.

    Connect your calendar (Airbnb, Vrbo, or Google Calendar) by pasting the ICS URL, such as `https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/yourcalendar%40group.calendar.google.com/private-abc123/basic.ics`.

    Review and save settings.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Basic Property Information" icon="home">
    Navigate to **Properties** > **Add New**.

    Fill in details:

    | Field          | Example Value                  |
    |----------------|--------------------------------|
    | Address        | 123 Maple Street, Austin, TX   |
    | Max Guests     | 4                              |
    | Amenities      | Wi-Fi, Parking, Pool           |
    | Check-in Time  | 4:00 PM                        |

    Upload photos and save. Your guest manual generates automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test the Guest Chat Interface" icon="message-circle">
    Preview your manual at the generated URL, like `https://alfiehost.com/manual/your-property-id`.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
        Open in Chrome or Safari. Ask: "What's the Wi-Fi password?" Expect: "Wi-Fi: GuestNet (password: Welcome123)"
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Mobile" icon="smartphone">
        Scan the QR code from your dashboard. Test voice input for hands-free queries.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    Embed on your site with this snippet:

    <CodeGroup tabs="HTML,React">
    ```html
    <script src="https://cdn.alfiehost.com/widget.js" data-property-id="prop_abc123xyz"></script>
    ```
    ```jsx
    import { AlfieHostChat } from '@alfiehost/widget';
    <AlfieHostChat propertyId="prop_abc123xyz" />
    ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
